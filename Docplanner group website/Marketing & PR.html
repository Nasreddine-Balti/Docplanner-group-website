Marketing & PR

Marketing & PR in Docplanner seems unique. Wonder
why? Because all of our projects are created
especially for doctors and for patients, too.

Marketing & PR roles

Lead Generation

The goal of this team is to interest doctors and facilities in
becoming Docplanner Customers. By using content marketing and
automation tools we educate and digitize doctors' offices.

Customer Experience

Their main aim is making our customers love us! :) They make our
customers engaged in Docplanner and other Dpralians products by
building great onboarding and nurturing. Everything is based on
analyzing Customers’ behaviour, data and testing :)

PR

The goal of our PR team is to build awareness and reputation of the
Docplanner as a brand.Our PR activities are aimed at customers,
users, journalists, investors or potentials and drive their decisions to
cooperate or support us. Because together we can do awesome
things that make us stand out from the crowd and give us a PR
power and competitive edge.

Patient Acquisition

‘I think our customers really like our products. And we're always
trying to do better’ Steve Jobs once said, and that’s the key for our
PA team. They still optimize our marketing activities to increase
patient booking numbers.
