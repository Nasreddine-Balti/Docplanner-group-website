Finance & Administration

In this team we support Docplanner’s life on a
day-to-day basis. Our goal here is to offer financial
information, organise the documents’ flow and to
make our office the best space to work;-)

Finance & Admin, HR, General Management roles

Finance

Finance is a strong team made of people with a high sense of
responsibility and organisation. So if you’ve always considered
yourself a well organised person with a great passion for numbers -
that’s the place for you.

Administration

host - you should become one of us.
